What is Avila?

Avila is a groundbreaking Web3 cryptocurrency built on a proof-of-stake consensus mechanism with advanced sharding capabilities. Our mission is to create a blockchain ecosystem that is truly scalable, energy-efficient, and accessible to everyone.

High Scalability

Avila's innovative sharding technology allows for thousands of transactions per second, solving the scalability issues that plague older blockchains.

Energy Efficient

Using proof-of-stake consensus, Avila consumes 99.9% less energy than proof-of-work cryptocurrencies, making it environmentally sustainable.

Secure by Design

Advanced cryptographic techniques and formal verification ensure that the Avila network remains secure against emerging threats.

Avila Technology

Avila leverages cutting-edge blockchain technology to create a platform that is secure, scalable, and sustainable.

Consensus Mechanism

Avila uses a novel proof-of-stake consensus algorithm called "Proof-of-Engagement" that rewards users for both staking and participating in network operations.

Sharding Architecture

Our dynamic state sharding technology allows the network to scale horizontally as demand increases, maintaining high throughput during peak usage.

Network Layer

Gossip protocol for efficient communication between nodes with adaptive routing based on network conditions.

Consensus Layer

Byzantine fault tolerant consensus with immediate finality, ensuring transactions cannot be reversed once confirmed.

Execution Layer

WebAssembly-based virtual machine for executing smart contracts with high performance and security.

Smart Contract Platform

Avila supports smart contracts written in multiple programming languages, making it accessible to developers from various backgrounds.

Interoperability

Cross-chain bridges allow Avila to communicate with Ethereum, Bitcoin, and other major blockchain networks, enabling asset transfers and data exchange.

Avila Tokenomics

The Avila token (AVL) is the native cryptocurrency of the Avila network, used for transactions, staking, and governance.

Token Distribution

Total supply: 1,000,000,000 AVL tokens

40% - Public Sale

Available to the community through various sale rounds with vesting schedules to ensure fair distribution.

25% - Ecosystem Fund

Allocated for grants, partnerships, and initiatives to grow the Avila ecosystem.

15% - Team & Advisors

With a 4-year vesting period to ensure long-term commitment to the project.

10% - Foundation Reserve

Reserved for future development, research, and unexpected opportunities.

10% - Staking Rewards

Allocated for staking rewards to secure the network and incentivize participation.

Token Utility

  • Pay for transaction fees and computational services on the network
  • Stake to secure the network and earn staking rewards
  • Participate in governance by voting on protocol upgrades
  • Collateral for decentralized applications and financial services

Inflation Model

Avila has a controlled inflation model starting at 5% annually and gradually decreasing to 1% over 10 years. This inflation funds staking rewards and ecosystem development.
